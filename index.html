How it works

Three steps to autopilot

1

Connect your store

Add your Shopify Token. OpenShop connects to your store's API and understands your catalog, orders, and customers.

2

Configure your agents

Choose which skills to activate — order management, customer support, inventory alerts, marketing campaigns.

3

Let agents run your store

Agents handle day-to-day operations 24/7. You get notified for decisions that need human judgment.
